How to know your electricity usage:

You can find your monthly electricity usage on your electricity bill. Look for the section labeled "Usage" or "Consumption" which will be measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh).

If you don't have a recent bill, you can estimate your usage by adding up the wattage of all your appliances and how many hours they are used each month. For example, a 100-watt light bulb used for 10 hours a day would consume:

100 watts * 10 hours/day * 30 days/month = 30,000 watt-hours/month or 30 kWh/month
